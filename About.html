About



Despite appearances, Jessica (in the hat) did not grow up in the 1800s. No, it was Georgia in the 1980s.

After years of feeling like an outsider (liberal nerd) and enjoying the escapism of movies and TV, she pursued a career in film, TV, and radio broadcasting and production. Celebrities were interviewed, film festivals were organized and attended, and life was pretty good, but it was about to get better.
